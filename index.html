Who qualifies?


We often hear people say that they aren't qualified to give other people feedback on their work.
But we believe that's incorrect.

Here's what you need...

Previous Experience

You do not need to be an expert from an F500 company to be able to help people still starting out in your field.

If you have the valuable experience that many beginners can benefit from, share it with people who need it.


Some time

There aren't any required hours, it depends on how much time you want to invest.


The more you are there for your students,
the more they'll love being part of your Space!


Openness

We believe that self-learners should have more accessibility to jobs, regardless of where they learned or where they're from.

Help make the internet a place that is non-discriminatory and filled with opportunity for everyone.
